FAQ

Your Questions Answered

Do I need a gym?

Not at all. The program is designed to work wherever you are. That said, access to a gym does open up more exercise options and equipment, which can accelerate your progress. Either way, we build the program around what you have available.

Do I have to commit to the full quarter?

No. While the program is structured on a quarterly basis for best results, you can start on a monthly subscription and upgrade when you are ready. There are no lock-in contracts and you can cancel anytime.

How does the program actually work?

Once you sign up, you get access to the HPU app where your fully customised program lives. Every workout is laid out for you — all you do is log in, follow the session and mark it complete. On my end I can see every workout you attend, track your consistency and make adjustments as needed. No guesswork, no figuring it out alone.

How do assessments work?

Assessments are done directly through the app. You fill in a questionnaire covering your health, lifestyle and goals. I then assign you a set of physical tests such as a wall squat, a Cooper run test and push-up test. You perform them, log the results and that data gives me a clear picture of your current fitness level and how to build your program from there. Assessments are repeated periodically to measure your progress.

I have never followed a structured program before. Is this for me?

Absolutely. In fact most clients start exactly where you are. The program is built around your current level, not where you think you should be. Whether you are a complete beginner or someone getting back into fitness after a long break, the program meets you where you are and builds from there at a pace that works for you.
